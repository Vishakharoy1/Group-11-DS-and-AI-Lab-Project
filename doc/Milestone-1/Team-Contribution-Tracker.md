# Team Contribution Tracker - Milestone 1

**Project:** Deep Learning-Based Human Face Authenticity Detection

This document tracks the work completed and responsibilities assigned for Milestone 1.

## 1. Rohit - Project Objectives & Problem Definition Lead

### Contributions in Milestone 1

- Defined and refined the project problem statement and overall project scope.
- Developed detailed project objectives aligned with the identified research problem.
- Identified the motivation, challenges, and real-world significance of AI-generated face detection.
- Prepared the introductory section and project objectives for the milestone report.

## 2. Raunak - Literature Review & Benchmark Analysis Lead

### Contributions in Milestone 1

- Conducted a comprehensive literature review on existing deepfake detection techniques.
- Studied CNN-based, Vision Transformer-based, frequency-domain, and noise-based detection approaches.
- Reviewed standard baseline models including XceptionNet, EfficientNet, ViT, Swin Transformer, and Face X-ray.
- Analyzed benchmark datasets such as FaceForensics++, Celeb-DF, WildDeepfake, and DFDC.

## 3. Vishakha - Research Findings & Comparative Analysis Lead

### Contributions in Milestone 1

- Compiled detailed research findings from recent deepfake detection literature.
- Collected and documented benchmark dataset statistics, model performance metrics, and supporting research sources.
- Performed a comparative analysis of existing deepfake detection approaches and the proposed hybrid framework.
- Identified research gaps and highlighted the key improvements offered by the proposed solution.

## 4. Aman - Baseline Performance & Evaluation Strategy Lead

### Contributions in Milestone 1

- Studied current baseline performance metrics using DeepfakeBench and other benchmark studies.
- Analyzed within-domain and cross-domain performance of state-of-the-art deepfake detection models.
- Designed the evaluation strategy, including benchmark datasets, performance metrics, and comparison methodology.
- Defined the proposed model evaluation framework and identified expected performance improvements over existing solutions.
